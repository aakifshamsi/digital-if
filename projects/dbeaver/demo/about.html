Who We Are

Built on Trust, Healing & Community

Serenity Spa was founded in 2012 by head therapist Maya Chen, who believed that quality massage therapy shouldn't be a luxury — it should be accessible, consistent, and deeply therapeutic.

Over twelve years we've grown from a single-room studio into a full-service wellness destination, without ever losing the personalized touch that made us Vancouver's favourite spa.

Every therapist on our team is RMT-certified with a minimum of 500 hours of clinical training, and we invest in ongoing education to stay current with the latest therapeutic techniques.
